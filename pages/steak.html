Prep time: 0-5 min

Cook Time: 10 min

Rating: 4/5

Steak is my go-to if I need a protein packed dinner, and I like to add some sides too to balance it out.

I'm not super great at cooking steak, but it's good enough to make me close my eyes and remember to never go vegetarian. I like to add sides of potatoes or onions or brussel sprouts after because they are really good at soaking up flavor and will taste like the excess steak oil and spices. This is my version of gym-bro chicken and rice boy dinner.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

MAIN

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Pat your steak dry and season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat up your pan with a little bit of oil until it's really hot.
  3. Gently place the steak away from you in the pan and sear on all sides.
  4. Once all the sides are seared, turn down the heat and let the steak sit and cook. Add the seasonings on (garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper) to taste.
  5. Put in some butter and once melted, gently use a spoon to scoop some onto the steak over and over again. Flip and repeat.
  6. Once the meat is cooked a little under your liking, you can take it out and let rest for 5 min (this further cooks it because it's still hot).
  7. At this point, you can either clean up or cook the sides if you wanted any. Cook it directly in the steak pan without washing or cleaning it to soak up the flavors leftover.
  8. Once everything is done, cut and enjoy!
