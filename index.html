Hi! I'm Shanshan, a 20 year old college student with not enough time...

I LOVE to eat, but even more, I love to cook!

Everyday, I try my best to cook at least one meal (usually dinner) as it gives me time to relax and reset. However, I often find myself with no time to cook and eat.

So, I improvise. I'll prep for 30 minutes then go to class, cook in bulk and eat the leftovers the next day, and make "one pot" recipes to minimize the number of dishes I need to wash after.

Here are some of my favorite meals and their recipes that I cooked recently when under the pressure of time!
