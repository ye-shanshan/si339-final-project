Prep time: 1 hour

Cook Time: 20 min

Rating: 5/5

Pizza is such a fun dish to make with friends, for friends.

Because you essentially can add anything you want, what makes this special is the homemade pizza crust! It's super simple yet soft and crunchy. I'll also provide instructions for how to cook/prepare different types of toppings.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

CRUST

PIZZA

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Start by preparing your pizza dough. In a small bowl, stir together 3/4 cups of warm water, 1 1/2 teaspoons of sugar, and 1 packet (1/4 ounce) of active dry yeast. Set aside for 5 minutes, until the yeast is foamy.
  2. Add in 2 cups of flour and 1 teaspoon of salt to the yeast mixture along with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Here you can also add in the garlic powder. Mix again until the dough forms into a ball. If the dough is too dry to form a ball, add water 1/2 tablespoon at a time until the mixture comes together. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead into a smooth ball.
  4. Brush a large bowl with the remaining 1 teaspoon of olive oil and place the dough inside. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm place to rise until the dough has doubled in size.
  5. Place nonstick parchment paper to your baking pan then place your pizza dough on it. Roll it out/flatten until it fills the pan.
  6. Using a fork, poke holes all over the dough so that when it bakes, it does not rise too much.
  7. Spread your sauce, cheese, and toppings! If you are adding raw meat, be sure to cook it in a pan first before baking. Leave room for the crust if you want it to be there. If there is crust, brush over it with butter and garlic powder to add that extra bit of flavor.
  8. Bake at 475 degrees F for about 15-18 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
  9. Let cool and enjoy!
