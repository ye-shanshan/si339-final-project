Stir Fry Shredded Potatoes is a classic Chinese childhood dish.

I ate this a lot growing up, so it has a special place in my heart. Because my parents came from both the Northern and Southern regions (mom in Shanghai and Beijing, dad from Shenzhen), I've had 2 different flavors of this dish (sour and savory). Potatoes are so versatile so this is also super easy and simple to customize into whatver you like! You can eat this plain with only potatoes or with lots of other stuff mixed in too! Here's my preferred method:

MAIN

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Peel your potatoes and cut into super super thin strips.
  2. (Optional) Let the cut potatoes rest in some cold water to get the starch out.
  3. Cut the rest of your vegetables or anything else you want to add in, if any, into thin strips to match the potatoes.
  4. Heat a pan with some oil and wait until it gets hot.
  5. Add in the sausage patties and break them apart until they are in small pieces.
  6. Once the meat is 70% cooked, add in your potatoes and bell peppers.
  7. Stir and sautee until cooked. Add in chili oil, and either soy sauce or vineger (depending on what you're going for) and mix.
  8. That's it! Enjoy!
