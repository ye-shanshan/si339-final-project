Poke Bowls! Similar to Chipotle and Hawaiian-inspired, this deconstructed sushi is so filling!

I feel like poke bowl is my college student meal because it literally is just a raw mixture of everything and everything. I feel embarassed to even call it a poke bowl but here's my struggle, low-effort comfort meal.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

MAIN

OTHERS

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Start by cooking your rice. Once it's ready, you can add sushi vingeer to make it sushi rice, but that's optional.
  2. Cut up all of toppings into small cubes or thin strips.
  3. Cut your fish or equivalent into small cubes. Optionally marinate (I use soy sauce and seseame oil)
  4. Combine everything into a bowl. Place rice in first, then everything else.
  5. Drizzle with Wasabi and Siracha Mayo. (This is literally just a mix of mayo and siracha sauce, you can get this at the store or make it yourself)
  6. Top with green onions, seseame seeds, tempura flakes, or whatever flaots your boat!
  7. Enjoy with some seaweed!
