Prep time: 15-20 min

Cook Time: 20-30 min

Rating: 4/5

Chicken and Gnocchi has quickly become one of my go-to comfort meals because it is so hands off to make!

This is a simple one-pot fridge-cleanout dish where I just mixed everything I liked together into a pasta because it is so versatile like that. You can add anything you want to be honest, but here's my variation that I ended up loving.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

MAIN

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Start by marinating your chicken in whatever marinade/sauce you typically like. Set aside for at least 15 min before air frying until fully cooked.
  2. Wash and cut your vegetables. I like to cut my onions into strips, brussel sprouts I cut off the stem and then halve them, bell peppers I julienne, and mushrooms into slices.
  3. In a pan, head up some cooking oil then add some minced garlic. Once the garlic's aroma has mixed with the oil, add in your brussel sprouts and mix.
  4. Sautee the brussel sprouts and add in your onions until they are about 80% cooked.
  5. Next, add in your pesto sauce and some milk or heavy cream and mix until it's the consistency you like.
  6. Add in the gnocchi, mushrooms, and Ikea meatballs (frozen is fine). Gnocchi cooks quick, so be sure to keep an eye on it! I typically wait until the sauce starts bubbling.
  7. Finally, crack an egg in and cover your pan so it cooks over the top too.
  8. Add in your chicken and enjoy!
